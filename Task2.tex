\section{Step 1: Ingesting Daily Data and Visualizing
Distributions}\label{step-1-ingesting-daily-data-and-visualizing-distributions}

Before downscaling the data, we must ingest the daily maximum
temperature records and visualize their internal distributions. This
exploratory analysis helps us identify trends, anomalies, and structural
skewness across the different weather stations in Sri Lanka.

\begin{Shaded}
